About Advocate Brahm Dutt

With an illustrious career spanning over four decades, Advocate Brahm Dutt has established himself as a distinguished legal professional specializing in Civil and Criminal Law. His practice is built on principles of integrity, ethical conduct, and unwavering commitment to justice.

As a Padma Shri Awardee, his contributions extend beyond the courtroom into significant social work and public welfare initiatives, particularly in RTI-related legal advocacy that empowers citizens.

Based in Faridabad, Haryana, Advocate Dutt has represented clients at senior levels of the judiciary, combining legal expertise with a deep understanding of India's judicial system.
